Communication and Documentation

The contracted agent will be responsible for initially explaining to the buyers, or sellers of the AssistantPro Coordinator's working arrangement with them. AssistantPro Coordinator will provide business cards to the agent to be given out to the buyers, sellers and lenders.

When the sale takes place, the AssistantPro coordinator will send out a Letter of Introduction.

Abstracts

AssistantPro Coordinator will help locate the abstract and have it sent to the abstract company of choice. If the agent obtains the abstract at the time of the listing or any time before the home sells, they should give the abstract to the AssistantPro Coordinator to send to the abstract company with instructions to hold for orders.

Loan Process

AssistantPro Coordinator will contact the Loan Officer to track the progress of the loan and secure written commitment on or before the committed date reflected on the purchase agreement. In the event the loan is denied for any reason, AssistantPro Coordinator will contact the contracted agent and the agent will proceed.

Appriasals

AssistantPro Coordinator will schedule and track the appraisal process and report to the contracted agent of any work required. The agent will handle any oral or written communication with the buyer, seller, or selling agent involving any negotiations of the work requirements.

Termite Inspections

AssistantPro Coordinator, when applicable, may order and schedule termite inspections. Any termite problems will be referred to the contracted agent.

Home Warranties

It is the responsibility of the AssistantPro Coordinator to call or email for the placement of the home warranty when applicable. AssistantPro Coordinator will notify the lender of the confirmation number and deliver to the lender a copy of the bill.

Title Opinions

AssistantPro Coordinator will assist the closing department to complete the documents needed to satisfy objections on the title opinion. AssistantPro Coordinator will work directly with the buyers, sellers or agents for the purpose of collecting information and communicating the same to the closing department. If there are any irreconcilable problems, AssistantPro Coordinator will deliver a copy of the title opinion to the contracted agent so a decision on how to proceed can be made.

Closing Documents

When the documents are completed, AssistantPro Coordinator will deliver them to the lender or closing department or agent, as required. The AssistantPro Coordinator will obtain the HUD Settlement statement as quickly as possible for the contracted agent and buyer or seller to review.
